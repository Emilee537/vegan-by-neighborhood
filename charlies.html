A Day At Charlie's

When I went to Charlie's, I was pleasantly surprised. Not only was the food super good, but everyone there was really nice. I mean, like, even pulled my seat out for me! I'm not one for weird chivalry, but this kind of gesture really started things off great. Anyway, food.

I ordered the Oscar Wilde salad (don't know why it's named after him, but OK) and the crispy potato wedges. Who can turn down a wedge? Not I.

The salad featured flaky tofu skins over a bed of quinoa and romaine. If you're a texture lover, this is it. Just get me an ASMR microphone and let me crunch all over it with this dish!
